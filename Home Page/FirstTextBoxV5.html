Wildlife

As one of one of the last untouched natural landscapes in Europe, North Uist is a haven for diverse wildlife, boasting a variety of bird species, marine life, and other animals. Unique habitats such as machair, unpolluted coastal areas and lochs, pristine beaches and undisturbed peatlands support an equally unique flora and fauna..
